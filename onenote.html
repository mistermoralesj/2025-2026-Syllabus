Accessing Your OneNote Class Notebook

After Morales shares the OneNote Class Notebook link, follow these steps to access and begin using your notebook

1. Receive the Link

Morales will give you a specific link to the OneNote Class Notebook for your course. This link is unique to your class and will grant you access to all notebook areas.

2. Open the Link

Click on the provided link. It will usually open in:

  • The OneNote desktop application (if installed)
  • Or in your web browser using OneNote Online

3. Explore the Class Notebook

Once the notebook loads, you'll see two main sections:

  • Content Library: A read-only space with resources and documents shared.
  • Your Personal Section: A private space that only you and your teacher can see. Use this area to complete assignments and take notes. Please remember that anything written here is visible to Mr. Morales for grading and feedback.

4. Navigate and Use

Familiarize yourself with the layout and use the notebook regularly to:

  • Read materials in the Content Library
  • Collaborate with classmates in the Collaboration Space
  • Complete and organize your work in your personal section

If you're new to OneNote or want more help, the following guides are a great place to start:

  • Microsoft Support: Create a Class Notebook
  • Quickstart Guide (Gulf Coast State College)
  • Using OneNote Class Notebooks (Microsoft)
  • Cloud Design Box: Learning Tools in OneNote
